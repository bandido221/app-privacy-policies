Babykicker

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: June 21, 2026

Overview

Babykicker is a fetal movement tracking tool for personal use. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is stored. We are committed to your privacy.

Data We Collect

Babykicker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data on its own. All information you enter into the app is stored locally on your device only, including:

  • Baby profile names and due dates
  • Kick counting session records
  • Movement activity logs
  • Wellness tracker items and history

No Internet Access

The app itself does not connect to the internet, does not use analytics services, and does not automatically send any data to external servers. No account or sign-in is required. The only way your data leaves the app is if you choose to export, email, or print it yourself, as described below.

Data Storage

All data is saved using Android's SharedPreferences storage on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all stored data, unless you have created a backup as described below.

Exporting, Backing Up, and Sharing Your Data

Babykicker includes optional, user-initiated tools for exporting your data. These actions only happen when you tap a button to trigger them — the app never does this automatically or in the background.

  • Backup & Restore: You can save a backup of your app data to a file in the "Babykicker" folder under Downloads on your device. You can later load that file back into the app, including on a different device. This file remains on your device unless you choose to move, share, or delete it yourself.
  • Reports: You can generate a daily, weekly, or monthly PDF report summarizing your tracked data. You may choose to view this report in the app, send it to a printer connected to your device, or share it using your device's email or sharing menu. The app does not send this report anywhere unless you actively choose to do so.

Once a backup file or report is shared outside the app (for example, through an email provider or printer), its handling is governed by that third-party app's own privacy practices, not by Babykicker.

Third-Party Services

Babykicker does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics platforms. Any sharing of exported data (such as through an email app or printer) only occurs when you actively choose to use your device's built-in share or print options.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by adults. It is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Medical Disclaimer

Babykicker is a personal tracking tool only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider with any questions about your pregnancy.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
